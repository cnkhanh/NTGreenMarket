# EP-04 — Donations

## Goal
Enable employees to browse participating charities and make donations in support of the event's charitable goals.

## In Scope
- Browse the list of participating charities
- View charity details (name, description, cause)
- Make a donation to a selected charity
- View donation confirmation
- Admin: add/remove/manage charities

## Out of Scope
- Real payment processing — donation mechanism (internal credits, pledges, etc.) TBD
- Donation receipts or tax documentation

## Roles Involved
- Employee (Buyer)
- Admin
